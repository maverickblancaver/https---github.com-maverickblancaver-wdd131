The Philippines

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian archipelago comprising over 7,000 islands, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant marine life, and rich biodiversity. Its culture is a unique blend of indigenous, Malay, Spanish, and American influences, reflected in its festivals, cuisine, and arts. The country is predominantly Roman Catholic, and its people are renowned for their warm hospitality and strong community spirit. The capital is Manila, while the largest city is Quezon City, both part of the Metro Manila region.
