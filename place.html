About the Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelago in Southeast Asia, comprising over 7,000 islands. It is known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and stunning natural landscapes.

Explore the diverse regions, from bustling urban centers like Manila to serene tropical beaches in Boracay and Palawan.
