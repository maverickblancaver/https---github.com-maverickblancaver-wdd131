Fitness and Wellness Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Fitness and Wellness Hub

This name was selected because it encapsulates the broad range of content related to fitness, nutrition, and mental wellness. It is straightforward and easily recognizable.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Fitness and Wellness Hub is to provide users with comprehensive resources on fitness routines, nutrition, mental health, and overall wellness. The site aims to support users in achieving a balanced approach to physical and mental health.

Scenarios

  • How can I create an effective 3-day split upper body dumbbell workout?
  • What are some healthy meal plans that support muscle gain?
  • What techniques can I use to manage stress and improve my mental well-being?

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

Blue (#0066cc) - Used for headings, footer, and accents
Light Grey (#f4f4f4) - Used for background color

Typography

The primary font for the website will be Arial, sans-serif. This font will be used for body text, headings, and navigation menus for its readability and clean appearance.
